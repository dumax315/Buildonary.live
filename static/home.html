How To Play

Controls:

When building with a mouse using these controls:

Left Click: Add Voxel
Shift + Left Click: Remove Voxel
Right Click: Orbit the Camera
Shift + Right Click: Pan the Camera
Scroll Wheel: Zoom In/Out

When building on moble, use 1 finger to place and 2 fingers to move the camera.

About

Buildonary.live is Voxel pictionary on the web. The game plays like Skribbl.io but with a 3D twist. You and your friends play rounds of alternating between creating 3D art to a prompt at the top of the screen and trying to guess what other people are creating. If you want to play with strangers, just click the join a public room button. If you make something cool make sure to click the save image as PNG and share it in the offical discord.
